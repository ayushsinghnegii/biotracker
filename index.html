Complete Project Overview

A presentation-ready explanation of what the project solves, how it works, and why it matters for biodiversity conservation.

!

Problem Statement

People often struggle to identify unknown plants and animals in real time. Traditional methods are slow, require expert knowledge, and do not provide an easy digital record for learning or conservation use.

◎

Project Objective

BioTracker AI aims to build a smart web platform that helps users capture, upload, identify, and document biodiversity observations with AI-powered analysis, educational insights, and downloadable reports.

⚙

Technology Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask backend, SQLite database, PlantNet API for plant detection, TensorFlow MobileNet for browser-side classification, ngrok sharing, and an admin management panel.

♥

Why Biodiversity Matters

Biodiversity supports ecological balance, food systems, climate resilience, and natural habitats. A smart identification platform can increase awareness and help people value and protect species around them.

⇱

Future Scope

The platform can be expanded with location-based biodiversity mapping, analytics charts, community observations, conservation status alerts, multilingual support, and advanced research datasets.

★

Project Impact

This project is not just an AI scanner. It acts as a biodiversity learning tool, digital observation diary, reporting platform, and management dashboard — making it suitable for both academics and real-world awareness.

Biodiversity Insights

Understanding and protecting the variety of life on Earth.

What is Biodiversity?

Biodiversity is the variety of all living things, including plants, animals, and microorganisms, and the ecosystems they form. It's essential for a healthy planet.

Why It Matters

Ecosystems provide us with essential services like clean air, water, food, and medicine. Biodiversity is the foundation of these services.

Threats to Life

Habitat loss, climate change, pollution, and overexploitation are major threats to global biodiversity. Many species are facing extinction.

How We Help

By making species identification easy and accessible, we hope to inspire curiosity and promote conservation efforts worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about BioTracker AI answered.

Do I need internet to scan animals?
No, the basic animal detection runs directly on your device, making it super fast. However, fetching detailed Wikipedia information does require an internet connection.
Can it identify any plant in the world?
We use the enterprise PlantNet API which contains over 40,000+ plant species globally. While it's extremely accurate, it might struggle with heavily edited or blurry photos.
Is my uploaded data safe?
Absolutely. We do not sell or share your scanned images with third parties. Your account password is also heavily encrypted.
